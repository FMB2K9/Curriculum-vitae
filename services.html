VOXI UK 80GB 5G Mobile Plan – £10 Monthly

Contract-free mobile operator VOXI, the virtual network provider (MVNO) sibling of Vodafone, has launched a new promotion that offers their entry-level £10 per month SIM-Only plan with 80GB of mobile broadband data (usually 20GB), as well as unlimited access to social media, calls, text and picture messages.

The plan, which also supports WiFi Calling, 4G Calling and Visual Voicemail, operates off a 30-day subscription and can also be taken as an eSIM. At the time of writing, VOXI has not set an end date for this 80GB special offer .
